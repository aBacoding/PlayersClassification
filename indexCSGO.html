ABOUT CYBERSPORT IN CS:GO

Esports is a team or individual competition based on computer video games. There are a huge number of games in this area, such as: Counter-Strike, Dota 2, League of Legends, PUBG, Fortnite, Valorant and other popular games that have their own rules. Each discipline has its own stars, for example, in Counter-Strike, Oleksandr "s1mple" Kostyliev is considered the best player of all time, and in Dota 2 one of the best is Johan "N0tail" Sundstein. In this website you will dive into the fascinating world of esports in the discipline of Counter-Strike : Global Offensive. Global Offensive has one of the most popular esport scenes in the world. The Global Offensive professional scene consists of leagues and tournaments hosted by third-party organisations, and Valve-sponsored tournaments known as Major Championships. Majors are considered the most prestigious tournaments in Counter-Strike circuit and have among the largest prize pools; originally announced at $250,000, the prize pools for Majors have risen to $1,000,000 since MLG Columbus 2016. After that, one of the largest prize funds grew to $ 1,250,000 at PGL Major Stockholm 2021, and this major tournament was won by Natus Vincere, who broke the record. Astralis is the most successful Global Offensive team of all time, with the core members of that team winning four Majors together.
